The Quiet Craft of Reading

He turned the page slowly, letting each sentence settle before moving on. There was no hurry here — only the deliberate pleasure of attention that reading, at its best, requires.

The margins filled with faint pencil marks, small harvests gathered along the way — a phrase, a turn, a word worth keeping.

Later, when the chapter was done, he would return to them one by one.

settle before moving on
在继续之前先沉淀、消化——不急着往下读。
deliberate pleasure
刻意为之、从容不迫的愉悦。
gathered along the way
一路走一路攒下来的。
开关逻辑：讲解态点蓝词 / 仅标记态点钮 → 抽屉滑出；再点钮（或抽屉边缘的 › ）收起。仅标记默认收起、讲解默认展开，但两态都能手动开关，记住你上次的选择。
