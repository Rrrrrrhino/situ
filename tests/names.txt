Defence Diplomacy at the Shangri-La Dialogue

Beijing dispatched a lower-level delegation to the Shangri-La Dialogue this year, a conspicuous downgrade from previous summits. Defence Minister Dong Jun stayed away, while US Secretary of Defense Pete Hegseth used his keynote to admonish China over its assertive posture in the South China Sea.

Analysts at the International Institute for Strategic Studies in Singapore noted that the absence of senior Chinese officials underscored mounting tensions. Jose Manuel, a researcher at Nanyang Technological University, argued that such snubs reverberate far beyond the conference halls of the Shangri-La Hotel.
